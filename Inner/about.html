About Anuttaram 3.0

🌟 Welcome to Anuttaram 3.0, the annual cultural fest of Government Engineering College Sheohar. A vibrant platform celebrating music, dance, drama, arts, literature, and creativity. Join us this year and be a part of unforgettable moments filled with art, entertainment, and inspiration!

✨ What's New in ANUTTARAM 3.0

🚀 Technical Competitions

Experience thrilling technical challenges including Hackathon, Blind Coding, Circuitrix, Robo Sprint, and Mathematics Race. These events test innovation, problem-solving skills, and engineering creativity.

🛠 Advanced Workshops

Participate in hands-on workshops on MATLAB, SolidWorks, AutoCAD, Proteus, and Generative AI. These sessions help students gain practical skills and industry exposure.

🎭 Cultural & Fun Events

Enjoy exciting cultural programs including Dance, Singing, Ramp Walk, Slam Poetry, and many fun competitions that bring energy, creativity, and entertainment to the fest.

🎵 Grand Musical Night

End the celebration with an electrifying Musical Night featuring energetic performances, vibrant lights, and unforgettable moments with friends and participants.

🏫 About the Institute

Government Engineering College, Sheohar is a prestigious public engineering institution in Bihar established in 2019 under the Department of Science & Technology, Government of Bihar. The institute is affiliated with Bihar Engineering University, Patna and approved by AICTE. GEC Sheohar is committed to delivering quality technical education and nurturing innovation, research, and leadership among aspiring engineers.

🔥 Fest Focus

ANUTTARAM 3.0 is organized to encourage students to explore new technologies, share innovative ideas, and develop practical engineering skills beyond their regular academic curriculum. The fest serves as a platform where students from different engineering branches participate in various technical activities such as coding competitions, robotics challenges, technical quizzes, project exhibitions, workshops, and seminars conducted by experts. These activities help students improve their problem-solving ability, teamwork, and creativity while gaining exposure to real-world technological developments. ANUTTARAM also promotes collaboration and interaction among students, faculty members, and industry professionals, creating an environment that motivates young engineers to innovate and contribute to technological advancement. Overall, the festival plays an important role in enhancing technical knowledge, leadership qualities, and enthusiasm for engineering among students.
